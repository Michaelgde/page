Music Sheet Editor (MSE) for GDevelop

What is MSE?

The Music Sheet Editor (MSE) is a powerful tool designed for GDevelop, allowing you to easily integrate and manage musical compositions in your games. With an intuitive interface and robust features, MSE is perfect for developers who want to add dynamic and engaging music to their projects.

Key Features

  • Compose and edit music directly within the editor.
  • Seamless integration with GDevelop projects.
  • Supports multiple instruments and tracks.
  • Export compositions to use in your games.
